Our Menu

Craving a caffeine fix? Kapehan's menu brews happiness with classic coffees, refreshing iced drinks, and delightful local favorites.

Matcha Latte

Matcha Latte is a refreshing cold drink made from finely ground green tea leaves, typically mixed with milk or milk alternatives, known for its smooth texture and subtle, earthy flavor.

Iced Latte

Iced Latte is a refreshing coffee drink made by pouring espresso or strong brewed coffee over ice and mixing it with milk or cream.

Caramel Macchiato

This exquisite beverage begins with a base of freshly brewed espresso, expertly layered with velvety steamed milk, creating a smooth and luxurious foundation.

Java Chip

The Java Chip drink is a coffee-based blended beverage typically made with coffee, milk, ice, chocolate syrup, and chocolate chips.
